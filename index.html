Our Story

The Taste of Authentic Telugu Home Food, in Every Meal

Amma Pakashala was born from a simple belief — that real food is the food made at home, by hands that care. In Kukatpally, Hyderabad, our kitchen keeps alive the soul of traditional Telugu cooking: slow-simmered curries, fragrant pulavs, and sweets that taste of celebration.

We don't cook like a restaurant. We cook like a mother would — measuring with love, seasoning with memory, and serving with pride. Every plate carries that warmth to your table.

  • Traditional Telugu recipes passed down through generations
  • Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients — never frozen
  • Authentic home cooking, not restaurant-style
  • Family recipes made with patience & care
  • Healthy, balanced homemade meals
  • FSSAI-licensed kitchen — hygiene you can trust
